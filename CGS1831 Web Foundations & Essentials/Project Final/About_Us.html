A Bit About Us!

Established in 2021 thanks to the help of the St. Pete Greenhouse small business center, The Angry Nerd Cafe has quickly become a must go spot in the heart of Saint Petersburg Florida. We pride ourselves as being a haven for nerds and the like. No matter what you geek out about, we are commited to providing you excellent coffee and a judgement free space where everyone is welcome! If you're not in a rush, feel free to stay a while & enjoy our free wifi for whatever you might want to do!
